# Android_PunchGame
Obtain a higher score in 30 seconds by tabbing the red button. You can upload your score with your name

### Google Play Store <br />
You can find the app on Googleplay store.<br /> link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nanya.a2019nanyanewyear&hl=en<br />

### In the Game <br />

When you start punching the red button, the game will start counting down. You can get one point with one punch in 30 seconds. There is a finish warning when the game is end. Click ok and then enter your name to upload your score.

### Score Upload to Google Firebase <br />

The score will then be uploaded to the Firebase realtime database.<br />
